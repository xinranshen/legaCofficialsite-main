A Megacy Manifesto

A Text Message

We have absolute control over our memories and thoughts.
Our body is the only medium that can control our identity.We accept the fact that humans cannot live forever.
We record our memories so that we can learn to say goodbye better.
We record memories, not to make us not cherish the present, to treat memories that can be viewed at any time as a placebo and to rely too much on them.
Memory is a precious heritage of mankind. Only by remembering our past can we have a bright future.
